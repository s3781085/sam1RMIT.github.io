Project Idea

This project will be an app which will help people that take prescription medication understand their medications better, it will inform them of the use, side effects ad any other important details about their medications. The app will also advise what over the counter medications users can or cannot take with their prescribed medication, this will ensure that the over the counter medication wont have an adverse effect on the perscribed medication. This app will also have having a set reminder to notify people to take their medication, if the user has to take the medication at a specific time this feture will ensure that they dont miss it.

Motivation

This project will be useful as in Australia approximately 9 million people are taking proscribed medication daily according to a recent survey of more than 1000 adult Australians. When this many people having to take prescription medication there is a need for people to understand the medications their putting into their bodies and why, but with the internet they often have to go to several different websites to find the information they want. This app will solve this problem by having all the information in one place, making it easier for users to access the information need.

Features

This application will show users information about the medication they search in the system. The information about the medication includes but is not limited to what it’s for, side effects, what its used for, precautions etc. This allows users to access the data they need in one place without having to scour the internet, which is more efficient for the user.

The application will also have a feature that enables the user to find out what can affect the over the counter medication can have on the prescription medication. For example, if the user has a cold, they may want to find out which over the counter cold medications they can take that doesn’t have a negative effect on the prescription medication they currently take. The features on the application will allow the user to either have their medications pre-saved in the app or enter their medications manually into the app, this will allow the user to be able to find out what can affect the prescription medications they take. This feature will include a listing option as well as a search box so the user can either enter what they want to compare the prescribed medication with or to list the best over the counter medications to take based on what they want to achieve, for example they can select congestion medication to see the list of best to worst congestion medications based on their prescribed medication.

The app will work in a way that show’s in the example of flu medication the feature will show the best to worst things the user can take for flu medications based on the prescription medication they take or if they enter in the search engine the brand and type of flu medication they want to uses, the app will tell them if its alright to take that specific over the counter medication or not.

Though this feature may have some drawbacks as it will be difficult making a database with the information needed to achieve this feature due to the shear amount of prescribed and over the counter medication sold today.

In the feature that shows users the best to worst medications to take based on users prescribed medication, there will be another feature that shows the user the rating of best to use to worst to use medications through pictures and colours, for example green happy face is fine to take and red angry face shows not to take with users prescribed medication.

The application will also have a notification system to notify users to take their medication at specific times. If the users have medication they need to take at a specific time, they can input it into the app specifying the time of the day they need to take it and what days e.g. every day, every second day etc.
